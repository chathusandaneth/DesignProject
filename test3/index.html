IOT Based Vehicle Monitoring System








By installing this system, your vehicle engine can be remotely turn on as well as the air conditioning system using an IOT technology. You can get the ability to cool the heated interior in the vehicle up to a specific level before arriving the vehicle. Also, there are another several features that can be obtained by this system in case of renting the vehicle to a third party, as GPS tracking of the vehicle to identify whether the vehicle exceeds a specific geomatic area. Also, an anti-theft alerting system is included in the system to clarify the security of the vehicle
